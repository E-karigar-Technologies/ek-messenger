How to find and invite contacts

You can identify which of your contacts use app when you sync them with your phone’s address book. Only contacts saved on your primary device will be available.

Find contacts

  1. Go to the Chats or Calls tab.
  2. Tap the search bar and enter the contact’s name or phone number.

Note: To avoid duplicate contacts, use your phone’s Contacts app to check if the number is already saved.

Can’t find contacts

If you can’t find your contacts, check the following:

  • Contacts are using this app.
  • Phone number is saved correctly.
  • Use correct international format.
  • Contacts permission is enabled.

Invite a phone contact

  1. Go to the Chats tab.
  2. Scroll to Invite to app.
  3. Tap Invite next to contact.
  4. Send invite link via SMS or apps.

More ways to invite

  1. Go to Menu (⋮) > Settings > Invite a friend.
  2. Select contact and send invite.
  3. Or tap Share link.

Chat without saving contact

  1. Go to Chats or Calls.
  2. Search using phone number.
  • If on app → Tap Chat
  • To save → Add to contacts
  • If not → Tap Invite

Can’t connect with a contact

If you can’t connect:

  • Check internet connection
  • Update the app
  • Switch Wi-Fi/mobile data
